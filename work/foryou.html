Carousell's For You page

we had a few problems...

  • High drop-off from liking to transacting
  • Low conversion of existing recommendation algorithms like Your Daily Picks and Recommended For You
  • The second tab which contained the Groups page was underutilised and underperforming

and we thought we could do this...

  • Show users their Likes again! They will be reminded of it, and transact on them.

but it turns out, we got it wrong...

  • We did discovery interviews to understand our users' behaviour around liking listings, and how they come to a decision to purchase.
  • We learnt buyers did not want to see their Liked listings again. There was a reason they did not transact the first time they saw it: perhaps the price was not good enough, perhaps the size was not right, perhaps the condition was too poor. Their liked listings were bookmarks for listings that came close to things they were shopping for. But they were still on the lookout for the perfect listing.

and so we went back to the drawing board...

  • We brainstormed different ways we could recommend new listings: based on their searches, based on the listings they chatted on, based on categories the listings they liked were from... etc.
  • And then we concept tested these with users based on their real browsing history. And our users told us which set of recommendations was still relevant for them. With that, we built a very vivid sense of which method worked best.

  • We worked with the Data Science team on the recommendation algorithm. They built a demo which we tested and gave feedback on, based on what we learnt from speaking to our users. We iterated and fine-tuned the algorithm multiple times.

and then released it into the world...

  • The a/b test was a success!
  • There was a 5.6% increase in percentage of users with listing views, and a 10.6% increase in percentage of users with quality chats.
  • That means users were browsing more, and chatting more, which were healthy inputs for transactions.
  • So we rolled this feature out to 100% of users and it's now live on the app!
